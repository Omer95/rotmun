About Us

Model United Nations Conferences are Simulations of the United Nations. At Model UNs, students where roleplay as International Policymakers, Leaders & Diplomats to devise ideas to resolve crucial problems that plague the globe, like Inter-Border Politics and Humanitarian Rights. The activity gives them a glimpse into real world politics and a chance to uncover how they operate, essentially making them aware of their Social Responsibility as a Global Citizen.

Rotaract Model United Nations, was inaugurated in 2009 to honor the consolidated efforts of the United Nations and Rotary International. It has since, year in and year out been operating at the highest echelon amongst Model UNs; with an amalgamation of the country's most talented individuals in attendance.

Rotaract Model United Nations has served for almost a Decade, the benchmark for Model UNs within the Country. Every year, Rotaract Model United Nations caters to Individuals and Institutions alike across the nation as a Platform to engage in Rhetoric and Discourse with regards to current and pressing issues present within the World. ROTMUN 2018, after nearly a decade of auspicious and illustrious Iterations, each having solidified ROTMUN as the quintessential Model UN currently functioning, offers prodigious academic value to its participating representatives as well as an opportunity to partake in cognizant dialogue for all involved.

With a Promise to serve as the greatest Iteration of Rotaract Model United Nations to date, ROTMUN 2018 promises to be the Standard, with its selection of Committees, the Veteran Model UN Experts of the years and the with a resounding Social Responsibility Program geared towards bringing about actual change.
