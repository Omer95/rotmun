Why ROTMUN

Core Focus

Our core focus has always been about the issues that our world faces; with determination and resolve, we help students formulate practical solutions to those issues.

Committee Chairs

Community

Venue: Institute of Business Administration, City Campus

The Institute of Business Administration is one of the oldest educational institutions of Pakistan and one of the first business schools in Asia. Modeled after the Wharton business school of the University of Pennsylvania, IBA has produced some of the most prominent business leaders of Pakistan. The city campus is located in the heart of Saddar, Karachi, with its Aman Tower looming over the MA Jinnah road, one of the busiest areas of the city. The campus is purpose builr and houses numerous classrooms and lecture halls, a state of the art library and the JS Bank auditorium, that can house more than 400 people.
